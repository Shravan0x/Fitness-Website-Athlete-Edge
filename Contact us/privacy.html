Our Role in Your Privacy

As a member or visitor of Athlete Edge, this privacy policy applies to you. This includes use of our website, mobile applications, and physical locations. We outline how we collect, use, and protect your information while you focus on achieving your fitness goals.

Our Responsibilities

When you visit our website or use our services, we act as the 'data controller' of personal data. For registered members, we process your data to:

  • Provide personalized workout programs
  • Track your fitness progress
  • Send relevant health and fitness updates
  • Manage your membership

We are registered with relevant data protection authorities and comply with all applicable privacy laws.

Your Responsibilities

  • Read and understand this Privacy Policy
  • Review your membership agreement which may contain additional privacy terms
  • Ensure you have permission to share any third-party personal information
  • Keep your account credentials secure

Data Collection & Usage

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Basic contact information
  • Fitness goals and preferences
  • Workout history and progress
  • Health metrics (with your consent)

This information is used to:

  • Customize your fitness experience
  • Improve our services
  • Communicate important updates
  • Ensure your safety during workouts
