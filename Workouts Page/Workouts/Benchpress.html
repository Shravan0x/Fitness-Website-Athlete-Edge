Bench Press

The Bench Press is a compound exercise that primarily targets the upper body muscles, including:

  • Chest (Pectorals)
  • Shoulders (Anterior Deltoids)
  • Triceps
  • Core (for stability)

This exercise is excellent for building upper body strength, improving pushing power, and enhancing muscle definition.

How to Perform Bench Press:

  1. Lie flat on a bench with feet firmly planted on the ground
  2. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width, palms facing forward
  3. Unrack the bar and position it directly above your chest with arms fully extended
  4. Lower the bar slowly to your chest while keeping your elbows at a 45-degree angle
  5. Pause briefly when the bar reaches your chest
  6. Push the bar back up explosively until your arms are fully extended
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions

Keep your core engaged and maintain proper form to avoid injuries and maximize strength gains.
