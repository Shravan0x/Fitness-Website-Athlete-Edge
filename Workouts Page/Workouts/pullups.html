Pull-ups

Pull-ups are a bodyweight exercise that primarily target the upper body muscles, including:

  • Latissimus Dorsi (Lats)
  • Biceps
  • Upper Back (Trapezius & Rhomboids)
  • Core (for stability)

This exercise improves upper body strength, enhances grip endurance, and helps develop a well-defined back.

How to Perform Pull-Ups:

  1. Grab a pull-up bar with an overhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width
  2. Hang with your arms fully extended and engage your core
  3. Pull yourself up by driving your elbows down and squeezing your back muscles
  4. Continue until your chin clears the bar
  5. Pause briefly at the top for maximum contraction
  6. Lower yourself in a controlled motion until arms are fully extended
  7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions

Maintain proper form and controlled movements to maximize benefits and reduce injury risk.
