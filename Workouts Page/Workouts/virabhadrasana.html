Virbhadrasana

Virabhadrasana, or "Warrior Pose," is a powerful standing yoga pose that strengthens the legs, opens the hips, and stretches the arms. It improves balance, focus, and stamina.

  • Legs
  • Hips
  • Arms
  • Core

This pose helps increase strength, flexibility, and stamina while promoting mental focus and determination.

How to Perform Virabhadrasana:

  1. Begin in a standing position, then step one foot back and bend the front knee.
  2. Extend your arms forward and backward, with the back arm parallel to the floor and the front arm reaching forward.
  3. Hold the pose for 30 seconds, then repeat on the other side.

Keep your hips squared and shoulders aligned. Focus on your breathing and maintaining balance.
