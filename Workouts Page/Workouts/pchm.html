Paschimottansana

Paschimottanasana, or "Seated Forward Fold," is a yoga pose that stretches the back, hamstrings, and calves. It also helps calm the mind and improve flexibility.

  • Back
  • Hamstrings
  • Calves
  • Core

This pose is excellent for relieving stress and promoting deep relaxation while stretching the lower body.

How to Perform Paschimottanasana:

  1. Sit on the floor with your legs extended straight in front of you.
  2. Inhale and lengthen your spine, then exhale as you fold forward, reaching for your feet or ankles.
  3. Hold the position for 20-30 seconds, then slowly come out of the pose.

Focus on lengthening your spine and avoiding rounding your back. Breathe deeply and relax into the stretch.
